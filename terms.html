Meet the Rustic Rooftop Teams

Certified Arborists

Our Certified Arborists are the backbone of Rustic Rooftop’s commitment to tree health and safety. With decades of combined experience, each arborist possesses deep expertise in tree biology, disease management, structural pruning, and emergency care. Accredited by recognized institutions such as the International Society of Arboriculture, our tree surgeons and experts employ advanced techniques and a passion for preserving natural beauty.

Emma Watson
Lead Arborist

Emma holds a Master's Degree in Urban Forestry and has managed hundreds of large-scale pruning and disease treatment projects. She is especially skilled in deep pruning to promote optimal canopy architecture and longevity.

Carlos Ramirez
Tree Health Specialist

Carlos specializes in tree pathology and pest management. His expertise ensures early detection and treatment of tree diseases, helping to keep urban and rural trees healthy and thriving.

The arborist team's continuous research and commitment to sustainable practices keep Rustic Rooftop at the forefront of tree care innovation and environmental stewardship.

Our arborists routinely partake in ongoing education and certification renewals to stay aware of the latest industry developments, including emerging pest threats, treatment protocols, and pruning innovations. They work closely with soil health teams to provide holistic tree care that nurtures both tree crowns and subterranean root systems.

Through comprehensive risk assessment and safety planning, the arborists ensure that all tree care activities maintain safety for both the environment and people. Utilizing state-of-the-art equipment and techniques like aerial pruning, cabling, and bracing, they protect and extend the life of every tree treated.

Soil Science & Ground Health Experts

Healthy soil is the foundation of robust ground ecosystems. Our Soil Science team includes experienced soil scientists, agronomists, and ecologists dedicated to understanding and enhancing the soil environment for maximum plant health and sustainability.

Dr. Priya Singh
Chief Soil Scientist

Dr. Singh leads soil fertility analysis and nutrient management programs. Her research focuses on organic soil amendments and bio-stimulants that naturally enhance root growth and water retention.

Liam Thompson
Soil Ecologist

Liam’s expertise lies in the microbial and chemical analysis of soils. He works closely with arborists to develop soil conditioning plans that promote symbiotic root-microbe relationships for long-term health.

The team employs the latest analytical equipment and field sampling techniques, ensuring every plot of land receives the precise care it deserves for thriving flora.

They utilize advanced lab and field technologies for comprehensive soil testing. This includes pH analysis, organic matter content monitoring, nutrient profiling, and moisture retention capabilities. These data guide the formulation of customized soil amendments that recondition compacted or degraded soils to support tree longevity and vibrant landscapes.

In collaboration with the arborist team, the soil scientists develop integrated soil-treetreatment plans that target root zone health holistically, fostering resilient root networks better able to source water and nutrients while resisting pathogens and environmental stresses.
