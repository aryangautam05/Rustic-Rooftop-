Privacy Policy of Rustic Rooftop

Welcome to Rustic Rooftop’s Privacy Policy. Your privacy is critically important to us. This document explains in detail how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Identification Information

Rustic Rooftop may collect personal information from you in a variety of ways including, but not limited to, when you visit our website, fill out a form, subscribe to our newsletter, or interact with our services. This personal data may include:

  • Full name
  • Contact information including email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Demographic information such as age, gender, and location
  • Payment information (processed through secure third-party providers)
  • Browsing behavior and site interaction data

1.2 Non-Personal Identification Information

Our servers automatically collect non-personal data such as your computer’s Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type, operating system, access times, and the pages you visit on our site. This data helps us improve user experience and diagnose technical issues.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our website and services
  • To improve and personalize user experience on our website
  • To respond to customer inquiries and service requests promptly
  • To send periodic emails including service updates, promotions, and newsletters
  • To process transactions in a secure and efficient manner
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

3. Sharing Your Information

Rustic Rooftop respects your privacy and does not sell, trade, or rent users’ personal identification information to others. We may share your information with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, provided those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

3.1 Third-Party Service Providers

Third-party providers such as payment processors, web analytics services, email service providers, and hosting companies may have limited access to personal data as required to perform their functions.

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your personal data if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities e.g., court orders.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Rustic Rooftop uses “cookies” and similar tracking technologies to access or store information. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us understand user preferences, gauge traffic patterns, and improve website functionality.

You may set your browser to refuse all or some browser cookies or to alert you when websites set or access cookies. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain parts of the site.

5. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures designed to maintain the safety of your personal information. This includes secure server technology, SSL encryption, regular monitoring, and access restrictions. Despite these efforts, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to protecting your data.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

You have rights under applicable data protection laws, including:

  • Right to Access: You may request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: In certain circumstances, you may request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can ask to limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Object: You may object to data processing for marketing or profiling purposes.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request transfer of your data to another entity.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via the provided contact information below.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our website and services are not directed towards children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate action.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy and comply with legal obligations. When we no longer need your data, we securely dispose of or anonymize it.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, and maintained on, servers located outside your country where data protection laws may differ. By using our website or services, you consent to such transfers as necessary for the service provision.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Rustic Rooftop reserves the right to update or change this Privacy Policy at any time. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes. Continued use of our website after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Types of Data Collected in Detail

12.1 Registration and Contact Data

When you register for an account with Rustic Rooftop or subscribe to newsletters and updates, we collect information including your name, email address, phone number, and postal address. This allows us to contact you, verify your identity, and customize your experience.

12.2 Payment and Transaction Data

For purchases, bookings, or service payments, we collect data related to your chosen services, billing information, payment details (processed securely), and transaction history. Payment processing is handled securely through trusted third-party payment gateways to protect your sensitive financial data.

12.3 Usage and Interaction Data

We use cookies and other tracking technologies to monitor how you navigate our site, your interaction with content, and preferences such as region and language. This data helps us tailor the website to your preferences, improve functionality, and measure effectiveness of our offerings.

12.4 Communication Data

Any messages or inquiries you send us, including emails, feedback forms, or calls, are collected to help resolve your requests and improve our services. We store this communication data securely and use it only for legitimate business purposes.

13. How We Use Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies help enhance website usability and performance. Below are types of cookies we use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for site functionality and login sessions.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect anonymous data about how visitors use the site to optimize user experience.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember user preferences such as language or region.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by third-party partners to deliver relevant marketing; these are controlled by you through browser or device preferences.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings but disabling certain cookies may limit functionality.

14. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites operated by third parties. Your interactions with these sites are governed by their own privacy policies. Rustic Rooftop does not control these websites and encourages you to review their policies before sharing personal information.

15. Data Protection and Security Measures

Rustic Rooftop employs comprehensive security protocols including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS)
  • Access control and authentication procedures
  • Regular software updates and security auditing
  • Data anonymization and minimization where possible
  • Employee training on privacy best practices and confidentiality

Despite best efforts, no security system is impenetrable. We continually assess risks to strengthen our protections.

16. Your Privacy Choices

You have choices over how your personal data is collected and used:

  • You may opt out of marketing communications by following unsubscribe links in our emails or contacting us.
  • You can configure browser or device settings to limit cookies and tracking technologies.
  • You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data as described earlier.

17. International Users

If you access our site from outside the United States, your personal information may be transferred and processed in the US. Data protection laws may differ. By using our services, you acknowledge and consent to these transfers.

18. Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your personal information, Rustic Rooftop will notify affected individuals promptly as per data protection regulations and take appropriate corrective steps.

19. Children’s Privacy Protection

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Parents or guardians who believe data was collected from their child may contact us to request removal.

20. Retention Policy

Personal data is retained only as long as required for business operations or legal compliance. When data is no longer needed, it is securely destroyed or anonymized to protect your privacy.
